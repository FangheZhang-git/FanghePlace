Scholarship Finder for High School Students

ScholarMatch helps high school students find scholarships that fit their academic profile, background, interests, financial need, and college goals. Instead of searching through long lists one by one, students can build a profile and see opportunities that are more relevant to them.

Find Scholarships That Match You

A good scholarship website should help students narrow down opportunities quickly. ScholarMatch uses information like GPA, SAT or ACT scores, intended major, state, citizenship status, first-generation status, household income, leadership, awards, and essay preferences to help students discover scholarship programs that may fit them.

Built for High School Students

ScholarMatch is designed for students who are starting the college funding process and need a simple way to organize scholarship discovery. The matching form keeps the process focused on information students usually already know or can easily look up.

  • Match by academic profile and coursework.
  • Consider financial need and background-based eligibility.
  • Save scholarships and return to them later.
  • Read and add comments about scholarship opportunities.

Start With a Free Profile

Students can create a free account, complete the matching form, and review scholarships that fit their profile. If you already have an account, log in to find scholarships that match you.
